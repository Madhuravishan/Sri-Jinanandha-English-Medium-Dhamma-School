Our Mission

Our mission is to impart the teachings of Buddhism in English, fostering discipline, mindfulness, and empathy in our youth. We guide children from primary grades up to the Daham Pasala Final Certificate, blending traditional Buddhist values with modern, accessible educational approaches.

Our dedicated teachers volunteer their time to ensure every child feels supported on their spiritual journey.
